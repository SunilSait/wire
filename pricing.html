FAQ

Pricing Questions

Are there any hidden fees?

Never. We provide a detailed quote before starting any work. The price you agree to is the price you pay — no surprises.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options for larger projects. Ask about our 0% APR for 12 months on qualifying installations.

What does the service call fee cover?

The service call fee covers the technician's visit, diagnostic assessment, and a detailed recommendation. If you proceed with the work, it's applied to the total.
