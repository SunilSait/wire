FAQ

Service Questions

How long does a typical rewiring project take?

A typical whole-home rewiring takes 3–5 days depending on the size of the home. We minimize disruption and clean up daily.

Do I need a panel upgrade for an EV charger?

Not always. We assess your existing panel capacity first. Many homes with 200A panels can accommodate an EV charger without an upgrade.

Can you work with my existing smart home devices?

Absolutely. We're experienced with all major platforms including Google Home, Amazon Alexa, Apple HomeKit, and Samsung SmartThings.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and schedule inspections on your behalf. It's all included in our service.
