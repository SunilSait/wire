Future Proof

Smart Home Integration

Click through our standard smart wiring integrations to see how VoltEdge upgrades your living experience.

Automated Lighting Systems

Whole-home lighting scenes, motion-activated path lights, and smart dimmer integration. Control any fixture using voice commands or scheduling.

Wiring Requirements:

Neutral wire retrofitting, smart switch relay installation, and low-voltage control lines.

Climate Synchronization

Integrate smart thermostats (Nest, Ecobee) with multi-zone dampers and ceiling fan switches to maximize home heating/cooling efficiency.

Wiring Requirements:

Common wire (C-wire) additions to HVAC control board, smart damper actuators, and relays.

Hardwired Smart Security

Hardwired smart cameras, smart locks, video doorbells, and perimeter sensor loops. PoE (Power over Ethernet) wiring ensures continuous operation.

Wiring Requirements:

Cat6 Ethernet lines to outdoor camera mounts, smart chime junction boxes, and smart lock power feeds.

Multi-Room Audio & Video

Structured audio wiring for in-wall speakers, home theaters, and centralized AV media racks. Share high-fidelity sound across rooms.

Wiring Requirements:

14-gauge oxygen-free speaker wire runs, hidden HDMI/optical lines, and central media rack enclosures.

Smart Panel Integration

Upgrade to a smart electrical panel (e.g. Span) to monitor electricity consumption per circuit in real-time, prioritize battery backup loads, and shut off breakers remotely.

Wiring Requirements:

Smart breaker panel switchover, ethernet connection, and home battery backup relay wiring.

EV Charger Load Balancing

Smart EV charging balancing limits current draw during periods of heavy household electricity use, avoiding panel overloads.

Wiring Requirements:

Smart current transformer clamp installation, local controller data lines, and Level 2 charging feeds.
