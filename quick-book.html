Booking Questions

How fast will the electrician arrive?

For emergency dispatches, our average arrival time is under 30 minutes. For routine work, you can schedule a convenient day and time window using the target date selector.

Are there dispatch fees?

We provide diagnostic visits starting at a flat rate. If you authorize us to perform the work, this diagnostic fee is fully credited towards your service total.
